St. Patrick’s Day food

\ ;;

For a St. Patrick’s Day feast with only green foods, try dishes like spinach dip, green mashed potatoes, pesto pasta, and a green salad with avocado. For dessert, enjoy mint chocolate chip ice cream or green frosted cupcakes!

St. Patrick’s Day activitest

Celebrate St. Patrick’s Day by wearing green, attending a parade, participating in a shamrock hunt, or trying Irish dancing.

St. Patrick’s Day origin

St. Patrick’s Day celebrates St. Patrick, the patron saint of Ireland, who is credited with bringing Christianity to the country in the 5th century. Originally a religious feast day, it has evolved into a global celebration of Irish culture with parades, music, and festivities.
