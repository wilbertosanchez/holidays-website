Pingxi Lantern Festival food

At the Pingxi Lantern Festival, you can enjoy a variety of traditional Taiwanese street foods, such as Tangyuan (sweet glutinous rice dumplings), BBQ skewers, Taiwanese sausages, and popcorn chicken. Other popular treats include oyster omelettes, bubble tea, and peanut brittle, which add to the festive atmosphere.

Pingxi Lantern Festival activites

The Pingxi Lantern Festival features the release of thousands of colorful sky lanterns, each carrying a wish for the new year. It also includes cultural performances, fireworks displays, lantern exhibitions, and interactive activities like lantern-making and calligraphy.

Pingxi Lantern Festival origin

The Pingxi Lantern Festival originated from the traditional practice of releasing lanterns to signal safety and communicate with loved ones during the Lantern Festival. The custom became more popular in Pingxi, a former mining town, where locals used lanterns to send messages during the Qing Dynasty, eventually evolving into the grand celebration it is today.
